# Privacy Policy for TidyTabs

**Effective Date:** March 16, 2024

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to TidyTabs ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("the App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: Email address and authentication data
- **Group Information**: Names of expense groups you create
- **Member Information**: Names of group members you add
- **Financial Information**: Expense descriptions, amounts, and payer details
- **User Content**: Any additional information you choose to provide

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

We collect minimal data required for the app to function:
- **Authentication Data**: 
  - Firebase Authentication user ID
  - Account creation timestamp
  - Last login timestamp
- **Document IDs**: Automatically generated identifiers for groups and expenses
- **Timestamps**: When groups or expenses are created or modified

We do NOT collect:
- Device identifiers
- Device type or model
- Operating system information
- Network information
- Analytics data
- Usage patterns or tracking data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Primary Purposes
We use your information solely for providing the core functionality of the app:
- User authentication and account management
- Creating and managing expense groups
- Processing and calculating expense splits
- Maintaining data consistency and security

### 3.2 We Do NOT Use Your Data For
- Analytics or usage tracking
- Performance monitoring
- Marketing or advertising
- User profiling
- Third-party data sharing (except for essential Firebase services)

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your information based on:
- Performance of our contract with you
- Your consent
- Our legitimate interests
- Legal obligations

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Storage Location

### 5.2 Security Measures
- Industry-standard encryption

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data:

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Withdraw consent
- Lodge a complaint with supervisory authorities

## 7A. Data Deletion Instructions

You can delete your data in two ways:

### Method 1: In-App Deletion
1. Open the TidyTabs app
2. Navigate to the Profile screen by tapping your profile icon
3. Scroll to the bottom of the screen
4. Tap the "Delete Account" button
5. Confirm your password when prompted
6. Your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted

This process will delete:
- Your account information
- All expense groups you've created
- All expenses and transactions
- All member information
- Any other user-generated content

### Method 2: Contact Us
If you're unable to access the app or prefer assistance:
1. Email us at jacobwebb@outlook.com
2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
3. Provide your registered email address
4. We will process your request within 30 days

After deletion:
- Your data will be permanently removed from our systems

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your data for:
- Active accounts: As long as your account is active
- Deleted accounts: Data is permanently deleted immediately upon account deletion

## 11. Third-Party Services

We use only essential Firebase services:
- **Firebase Authentication**: For secure user account management

- **Firebase Security Rules**: For data access control

We do NOT use:
- Firebase Analytics
- Firebase Performance Monitoring
- Firebase Crashlytics
- Firebase Cloud Messaging
- Any other Firebase or third-party services

For more information about Firebase's privacy practices, please review Google's Privacy Policy for Firebase: [https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
